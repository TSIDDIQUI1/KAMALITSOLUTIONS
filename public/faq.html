Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and processes. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications may take 2-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

How much do your services cost?

We offer competitive pricing tailored to each project's requirements. Our pricing is transparent and based on the scope of work, complexity, and timeline. Contact us for a free quote, and we'll provide a detailed proposal based on your specific needs and budget.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. Thanks to modern communication tools like video conferencing, email, and project management platforms, we can collaborate effectively regardless of location. We've successfully delivered projects for clients across Europe, North America, and Asia.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! We offer various support packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. Our support services include 24/7 maintenance, regular updates, technical support, and performance monitoring. We can customize a support plan based on your specific needs.

What technologies do you use?

We use a wide range of modern technologies depending on project requirements. Our expertise includes React, Vue.js, and Angular for frontend development; Node.js, Python, and PHP for backend development; AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud for cloud solutions; and various databases including MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB.

How do you ensure website security?

Security is built into our development process. We follow industry best practices including secure coding standards, regular security audits, SSL implementation, data encryption, and compliance with relevant security standards. We also offer comprehensive cybersecurity services for additional protection.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach, ensuring they look and function perfectly on all devices including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. We test across multiple browsers and devices to ensure optimal user experience everywhere.

Do you offer SEO services?

Yes! We incorporate SEO best practices into all our web development projects. Our SEO services include on-page optimization, page speed optimization, mobile optimization, schema markup, and technical SEO audits. We can also provide ongoing SEO services to help improve your search rankings.
